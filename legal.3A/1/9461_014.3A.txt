 Section 2.04.  Restrictions on Voting and Ownership of Stock.
Except as provided in NRS 89.070(2) and (3), no person may be a
shareholder in the Corporation or vote any shares of stock of the
Corporation, whether by voting trust agreement or otherwise, unless
such person is licensed to practice in the State of
Nevada.

at the designated time shall not work a forfeiture or dissolution
