 3.04. The adjusted minimum rent shall be due and payable
for each month commencing with the first month of the second year
of this Lease.  However, pending the determination of the adjusted
minimum rent, Tenant shall continue to pay minimum rent in the same
amount as the minimum rent required for the preceding lease year.
When the adjusted minimum rent has been determined, Tenant,
concurrently with the next monthly rent payment due and payable
after the furnishing by Landlord to Tenant of the computation of
the adjusted minimum rent, in addition to the adjusted minimum rent
for such month, shall pay Landlord a sum equal to the amount of the
increase in the minimum rent due for each of the previous months in
the lease year.
 3.05. If at the time required for the determination of
the adjusted rent, the Consumer Price Index above-mentioned is no
longer published or issued, the parties shall use such index as is
then generally recognized and accepted for similar determinations
of purchasing power.
 3.06.  The minimum rent shall be paid to Landlord in
advance on the first day of each month during the term of this
Lease.  Rent for any fractional month shall be paid on the
commencement date.
