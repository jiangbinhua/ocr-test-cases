ORDER DISMISSING VOLUNTARY PETITION

 The motion of the Debtors, DEOGRACIAS AND AURORA
GONZALES dba D & A APARTMENTS, to dismiss the voluntary petition
for relief under Chapter 11 of Title 11 of the United States
Code having been filed on April 3, 1992, Debtors'  attorney,
JOSHUA M. LANDISH, ESQ., in support of such motion, the Court is
of the opinion that dismissal of this case is in the best
interest of the Debtors and of their creditors and no opposition
being filed, it is therefore,

 ORDERED that the order for relief resulting from the
filing of the voluntary petition of April 3, 1992, be set aside
and vacated.
