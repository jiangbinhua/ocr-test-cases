AMENDED REAFFIRMATION AGREEMENT

 This agreement is made by General Motors Acceptance Corp.
("Creditor") and Lena E. Chandler (the "Debtor"), pursuant to 11
U.S.C. ~524(c).  The parties state that:

 1. On December 27, 1994, Debtor filed a petition for relief
under Chapter 7 of the Bankruptcy Code.

 2. At the time of the filing, the Debtor was indebted to the
Creditor in the amount of $8,438.28, because of a Retail
Installment Contract dated April 28, 1994.  The agreement is
attached as Exhibit A.

 3. The Debtor desires to repay the contract and to retain
possession of the collateral, even though she filed the petition in
bankruptcy.
