Statement Of Appellate Jurisdiction

The bankruptcy court had subject matter jurisdiction over this case pursuant to 28 U.S.C.
~~~1334 and 157(b)(1) and over the confirmation process as a core proceeding under 28 U.S.C.
~~~157(b)(2)(L).  This Court has appellate jurisdiction pursuant to 28 U.S.C. ~~158(a), as this is an
appeal from a final order confirming a plan of reorganization and from a final order entered post
confirmation which determines the rights and liabilities of parties subject to the confirmed plan.

Applicable Standard Of Appellate Review

The bankruptcy court's findings of fact are reviewed for clear error and conclusions of law
are reviewed de novo. In re Johnston, 21 F.3d, 323 (9th Cir. 1994); In re Tucson Self Storage,
Inc., 166 B.R. 892 (9th Cir. BAP 1994).
